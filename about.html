About Us

OctoTalks is...

an open-source news website utilizing the power of firebase/google cloud, python, SQL, AI, and UX tools to further advance credible news in the communication of complex subjects. Using our vast experience in python and HTML we crafted a website to breakdown complex subject matter to educate a larger population of people. We use AI models to analyze articles to check for credibility and then summarize the articles for easier content consumption.
