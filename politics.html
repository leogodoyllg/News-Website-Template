Bengal political violence: TMC's Mahua Moitra responds to BJP's charges.

Written by Nana Thomas. September 13, 2019
Politics

In an exclusive interaction with India Today, Trinamool Congress MP Mahua Moitra responded to questions about the post-poll violence in Bengal and allegations the BJP is making against her party.!

BJP is doing exactly what it did last 12 months. We're suffering from Covid. Till the MCC [Model Code of Conduct] was in force, we could do nothing. But, now, we have started work. On the other hand, Dilip Ghosh and other BJP MPs are doing a dharna in Kolkata. Ninety-five percent of what's going on in social media is fake. And the people of Bengal have already slammed the BJP-says Mahua Moitra
